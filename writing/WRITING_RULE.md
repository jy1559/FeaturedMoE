# RouteRec Writing Rules — Index

Writing guidelines are split into two files:

## WRITING_CORE.md
**Give this to the LLM at the start of every writing session.**
Contains: what RouteRec is, canonical terminology, first-use definitions, sentence-level rules, never-do list, experiment rules, pre-submission checklist.
~2 pages. Designed to fit in context without waste.

## WRITING_DETAIL.md
**Reference when: discussing motivation, planning section structure, or doing deep revision.**
Contains: motivation philosophy + requirements + current working storyline, motivation vocabulary, full paper structure map, section responsibility rules, definition style guide.
Not needed for routine paragraph-level writing tasks.

---

How to use:
- Routine writing (drafting a subsection, revising sentences): give WRITING_CORE.md only.
- Motivation or structure work: give both files.
- Updating motivation/structure plans: edit WRITING_DETAIL.md. Core rules in WRITING_CORE.md rarely need to change.
